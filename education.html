Education and Training

Education and training in the Center exists at multiple levels, with a primary focus on biomedical engineering design, development, enterprise and entrepreneurial training and mentoring. The Center coordinates with several degree and training programs within Dartmouth's Thayer School of Engineering and the Geisel School of Medicine, including undergraduate internships and post bachelor's or pre-med internships, and advanced training in the Master, Doctoral and Post-Doctoral Fellow experiences.

Collaborative research is ongoing with a large number of collaborating physicians in Surgery, Radiology, Medicine, Orthopaedics, Dermatology, and Emergency Medicine.

Special opportunities exist for admission to the engineering PhD Innovation Program, Surgical Innovation Training, MD/PhD Program, and Medical Physics PhD Education Program for qualified doctoral applicants and current students.  

National outreach opportunities exists in software development and training through ongoing collaborative projects.
