Deep tissue molecular sensing in vivo

Center Investigators work at the forefront of deep tissue imaging with optics, electrical impedance, microwaves, ultrasound, photoacoustics, magnetic resonance imaging and x-ray CT. Research projects are based around developmental design of new devices or original applications for existing tools.   

Instrument fabrication, instrument adaptation, software design and customization, along with image and data analysis and display strategies are all being developed in each of the imaging labs.
